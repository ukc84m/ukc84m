UKC 84M - SAN MATEO CHAPTER

The UKC also known as United Kabalikat Civicom in San Mateo was not yet establish during the past year in Province of Rizal. A fellow brother from Sta Rosa Laguna Chapter was able to endorse and sponsor the chapter of San Mateo.
Mr. Stephen Flores an NTC Licensed Radio Operator and a member of Philippine Amateur Radio Associations (PARA) is pursuing to have a chapter of United Kabalikat Civicom in San Mateo. During those year Mr. Flores was able to ask permission to join the KABALIKAT CIVICOM in Barangay Sta Ana. at that time the request to join was not granted due to the fact that its members were busy and did not have time for whatever reason. In connection Mr. Flores met a member of UNITED KABALIKAT CIVICOM Mr. Dexter Zaragoza-36M45 and presented to fellow brother Triskelion Mr. Vladimir Dungo 36M01 Chapter President of Sta Rosa Chapter to set an appointment in creating a chapter of UKC in San Mateo Rizal. Luckily the request was permitted to undergo legal orientation to the National Officers of UNITED KABALIKAT CIVICOM.
With the help of fellow brothers from the 36M Sta Rosa Laguna Chapter along with Governor Mark Selisana and UKC National President Jimmy "Buboy" Soriano it was legally placed in the San Mateo area, Rizal on November 7, 2019 (Thursday) in Moncarlo Subdivision Covered Court, Barangay Ampid 1, San Mateo, Rizal.
The United Kabalikat Civicom San Mateo Rizal was chartered on August 29, 2020 at Kambal Road Guitnang Bayan 1 San Mateo Rizal.
